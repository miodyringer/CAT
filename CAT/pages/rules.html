📜 CAT Game Rules 📜

🎯 Objective

The goal is to be the first person to move all of its cats from the starting area, completely around the board into the designated finishing zone.

🃏 Cards

Normal Cards (blue background) are used to move a cat forward along the track, the value printed on the card determines the range. Special Cards (red background) provide unique abilities, they are needed to start the game and can bring a strategic situation to the game.

  • ▶️Start Card: Needed to move a cat from the starting area onto its starting space on the track and move the designated value. Can also be as a normal card to move forward.
  • 🃏Joker Card: Can be played as a substitute for any other card in the deck (both normal and special).
  • 🔁Swap Card: Choose one of your cats on the track and swap its exact position with any other cat on the track. Pawns in the starting area or finishing zone are excluded.
  • 🔥Inferno Card: Choose to split the value of this card among the number of cats and burn any enemy cat that it passes over, sending them back to their starting areas.
  • ↔️Flex Card: Choose to move either forward or backward by the cards designated value.

🎮 Gameplay & Movement Rules

Basic Movement and Board Spaces

Each space on the track can only be occupied by one cat at a time. Cats are moved clockwise around the board. If a move ends by landing on a tile occupied by an enemy cat, then the enemy cat is going to get kicked into the designated starting area again.

The Start Tile

A cat that is occupying its own colored start tile is in a safe zone. The cat is immune and cannot be captured, swapped or burned. Furthermore, the cat cannot be passed over by any other cat. It serves as a solid wall, no cat can pass it until it is moved. To move into your finishing zone your starting tile can't be blocked because you move over this tile.

Entering and Moving in the Finishing Zone

Cats inside the finishing zone cannot be jumped over, they create a blockade for any cats behind them. The finishing zone needs to be filled sequentially. A cat must enter the finishing zone with an exact value. If a cat completes a full lap passing or landing exactly on the entrance to the finishing zone. It must complete another full lap around the board to get another chance.

🌊 Flow of the Game

The game is played over a series of rounds. At the beginning of each round, every player receives a number of cards that change each round in a descending cycle. Starting with 6 cards, descending to 2 cards. After the round with 2 cards, the cycle resets and the next round starts again with 6 cards. The game proceeds clockwise. On your turn, you must perform the following actions:

  • Select one card from your hand.
  • Play the card, moving one of your cats according to the value or special effect.
  • If you are unable to make any legal move with the cards in your hand, you drop all your cards and are skipped until the next round starts.
  • When all players had their cards laid down, the round is over and the next one begins until a winner is determined.

👑 Winning the Game

Bring all cats from the starting area into your finishing zone.
